How do I add Google Analytics to my site?

October 26, 2021

md2blog has no built-in support for adding trackers to sites it generates, and there are no plans to add such support.

Frankly, as a reader, I'd prefer that you not add invasive tracking to your site. GoatCounter might be fine.
